Cyber Security and Penetration Testing

As part of my studies and hands-on labs, I have developed a strong interest in cybersecurity, with a particular focus on ethical hacking and penetration testing. I’ve worked with various tools in Kali Linux to simulate attacks, assess system vulnerabilities, and improve defensive measures.
